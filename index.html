We are candlelit



Hello! This website is a project that's been stuck in my head ever since I found out how hard it can be and how much it feels like randomness when you're trying to publish a poem. I wanted to make a much simpler way for poets to publish, as well as provide a way to support the charities that are important to us. Thus, candlelit was born (with a big kick from n&w s5). This is still in heave development, so please, have patience, and enjoy your reading!


- Spencer Hill
